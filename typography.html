        The spirit flown forever!
    Let the bell toll! — A saintly soul
        Glides down the Stygian river!

                    Hast thou no tear?
                        Weep now or nevermore!
                    See, on yon drear
                   And rigid bier,
                       Low lies thy love Lenore!
